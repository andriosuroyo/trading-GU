SCENARIO: 3 Active Recovery Trades (SINGLE LAYER MODE)

+----------------------------------------------------------+
|  RGU - Recovery Manager         [Status: ACTIVE]         |
+----------------------------------------------------------+
| #  | Dir | OpenPrice | DistTo | RemTime  | Opp | Status  |
+----------------------------------------------------------+
| 1  | BUY | 4559.09   | 516    | 1:47:23  | 2   | ACTIVE  |
| 2  | BUY | 4338.41   | 2847   | 0:23:45  | 8   | WARN    |
| 3  | SELL| 4564.25   | 4523   | 0:04:12  | 11  | CRIT    |
+----------------------------------------------------------+
|  Active: 3 | Pending: 0 | Closed Today: 12              |
+----------------------------------------------------------+

DistTo Calculation Examples:
  Row 1: Target=4559.09, Current=4564.25 -> |4559.09-4564.25|*100 = 516 points
  Row 2: Target=4338.41, Current=4310.00 -> |4338.41-4310.00|*100 = 2841 points (showing 2847)
  Row 3: Target=4564.25, Current=4519.02 -> |4564.25-4519.02|*100 = 4523 points

Note: DistTo always shows INTEGER (no decimals)

SCENARIO: LAYERED MODE (Multiple entries on same recovery)

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  RGU - Recovery Manager - LAYERED MODE      [Status: ACTIVE]          |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| #  | Dir | Target  | L1@    | L2@    | L3@    | TotalDist | Status   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 1  | BUY | 4000.00 | 3985.0 | 3970.0 | --     | 4500      | ACTIVE   |
| 2  | SELL| 4100.00 | 4125.0 | --     | --     | 2500      | WARN     |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
|  Active: 2 | Total Layers: 3 | Avg Entry: 3987.5 / 4112.5           |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

Layer Mode Explanation:
  Row 1 (BUY Recovery):
    - Original loss: BUY @ 4000.00, SL hit @ 3990.00
    - Layer 1: GU opened BUY @ 3985.00 -> RGU entered @ 3985.0
    - Layer 2: GU opened BUY @ 3970.00 -> RGU entered @ 3970.0
    - Target: 4000.00 (magnet)
    - TotalDist: 1500 (L1) + 3000 (L2) = 4500 points potential
    
  Row 2 (SELL Recovery):
    - Original loss: SELL @ 4100.00, SL hit @ 4110.00
    - Layer 1: GU opened SELL @ 4125.00 -> RGU entered @ 4125.0
    - No Layer 2 yet (price didn't move far enough)
    - Target: 4100.00
    - TotalDist: 2500 points potential

Status Logic (Most Dire Wins):
------------------------------

Example 1: RemTime OK (1:47:23 = white), Opp LOW (2 = white) -> ACTIVE (white)
Example 2: RemTime WARN (0:23:45 = yellow), Opp HIGH (8 = yellow) -> WARN (yellow)  
Example 3: RemTime OK (0:04:12 = red), Opp CRIT (11 = red) -> CRITICAL (red)

Color Legend:
-------------
Background: Dark (20,20,20)
Text: Light gray (220,220,220)
BUY (Dir column): Green (50,200,50)
SELL (Dir column): Red (200,50,50)
DistTo: White (always positive integer)
Active: White
Warning: Yellow (255,200,0)
Critical: Red (255,50,50)
Closed: Gray (100,100,100)

Font: Monospace for alignment
Update frequency: Every 1 second
